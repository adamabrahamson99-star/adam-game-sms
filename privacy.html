Privacy Policy

Program Name: Adam's Game SMS Program

This SMS program is a private, opt-in social game for a friend group. The program sends occasional game-related text messages to participants who have agreed to participate.

Information Collected

The program may collect participant names, mobile phone numbers, game participation status, and basic message responses such as OPTIN, OPTOUT, HELP, and STOP.

How Information Is Used

Participant information is used only to operate the private friend-group game, send game start messages, process game participation responses, send challenge messages to opted-in participants, and manage removal requests.

Data Sharing

Mobile phone numbers, SMS consent, and participant information are not sold, rented, shared, or disclosed to third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

Opt-Out

Participants may reply STOP at any time to be permanently removed from the player list. Participants may also reply HELP for help.

Contact

For questions about this SMS program, contact the organizer directly.
