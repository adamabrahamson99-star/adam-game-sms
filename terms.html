Terms and Conditions

Program Name: Adam's Game SMS Program

Adam's Game SMS Program provides low-volume, opt-in automated SMS notifications for an event-based social game. Participants may receive game start messages, participation confirmation messages, and automated challenge notifications while a game session is active.

Opt-In

Participants consent by directly providing their mobile phone number to the organizer and agreeing to receive automated SMS notifications for the game program. Before each game session, participants may reply OPTIN to participate in that session or OPTOUT to skip that session. Only participants who reply OPTIN are eligible to receive challenge notifications during that game session.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. During active game sessions, participants may receive occasional automated SMS notifications between 7 PM and midnight. Some hours may not result in a message.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply depending on the participant's mobile carrier and phone plan.

Opt-Out

Participants may reply STOP at any time to be permanently removed from the player list and stop receiving messages from this SMS program.

Help

Participants may reply HELP for help or contact the organizer directly.

Safety

Challenges are optional. Participants may ignore any challenge they do not want to complete. Participants are expected to follow all laws, venue rules, and personal boundaries.

Contact

For questions about this SMS program, contact the organizer directly.
