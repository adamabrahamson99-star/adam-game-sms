Program Name: Adam's Game SMS Program

Adam's Game SMS Program is a private, opt-in social game for a friend group. Participants may receive game start messages, participation confirmation messages, and occasional challenge messages while a game session is active.

Opt-In

Participants join by directly providing their mobile phone number to the organizer and agreeing to participate. Before each game session, participants may reply OPTIN to play that night or OPTOUT to skip that night.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. During active game sessions, participants may receive occasional messages between 7 PM and midnight. Some hours may not result in a message.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply depending on the participant's mobile carrier and phone plan.

Opt-Out

Participants may reply STOP at any time to be permanently removed from the player list. After replying STOP, participants will no longer receive messages from this program unless manually re-added by the organizer after requesting to participate again.

Help

Participants may reply HELP for help or contact the organizer directly.

Eligibility and Safety

Challenges are optional. Participants may ignore any challenge they do not want to complete. Participants are expected to follow all laws, venue rules, and personal boundaries.

Contact

For questions about this SMS program, contact the organizer directly.
